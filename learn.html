About Ocean Pollution

Marine pollution occurs when man-made contaminants including plastics, debris, heavy metals, and chemical waste makes it's way into bodies of water. This causes severe damage to ocean ecosystems, and the human communities that depend on them. Learn more about our oceans, and how they're affected by manmade pollution.
