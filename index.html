Mission Blue

Be the generation that ends the careless dumping of plastics and waste into the ocean. Every piece of garabage counts up and your effort is what makes a difference.

Our Goal

The oceans form the world's largest carbon sink and contains rich biodiversity that supports many communities. Ocean pollution is a major problem that threatens the livelihood of generations and exacerbates climate change. Our goal is to raise awareness on the state of ocean pollution today and inspire ordinary people to take simple actions to protect the oceans.
